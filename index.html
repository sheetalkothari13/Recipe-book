The Art of Making Pancakes

ğŸ´ Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

ğŸ³ Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the milk, egg, and melted butter, and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and grease it lightly.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden.
  6. Serve with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings!
